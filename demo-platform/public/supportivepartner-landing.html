🤝 Essential Partner Combinations

💑🌸
SupportivePartner + MenoWellness
The essential combination for couples navigating menopause together. While she tracks symptoms, you learn how to provide the right support at the right time.
  • Synchronized symptom awareness and support
  • Menopause education for better understanding
  • Communication tools for difficult conversations
  • Intimacy guidance during hormonal changes
  • Crisis support for severe symptom days
💑🌱
SupportivePartner + FertilityTracker
Perfect for couples trying to conceive. Coordinate timing, understand the emotional journey, and provide support during the fertility process.
  • Fertility timing coordination and planning
  • Emotional support during two-week waits
  • Understanding fertility stress and pressure
  • Celebration of milestones and progress
  • Professional fertility counseling integration
💑🧘
SupportivePartner + SoberPal
Essential support for partners of those in addiction recovery. Learn how to help without enabling, recognize triggers, and maintain healthy boundaries.
  • Understanding addiction and recovery process
  • Healthy boundary setting and maintenance
  • Crisis intervention and relapse prevention
  • Family healing and communication strategies
  • Support group connections for partners
💑🏥
SupportivePartner + Provider Dashboard
Healthcare providers can see how partner support affects patient outcomes and provide guidance for both individuals in the relationship.
  • Partner involvement in treatment planning
  • Relationship health impact on medical outcomes
  • Couples therapy and counseling referrals
  • Crisis intervention with partner context
  • Holistic care approach including relationships
